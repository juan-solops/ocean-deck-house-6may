Abades — Tenerife's Best Kept Secret

Abades is a small, authentic fishing village on the south-east coast of Tenerife. No mega-resorts, no crowds — just crystal-clear water, world-class snorkeling, and the ruins of the mysterious Sanatorio de Abona nearby.

The village sits in a natural bay protected from wind, making it one of the calmest swimming spots on the island year-round.
