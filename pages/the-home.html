The Home

Your fully private holiday house in Abades, South Tenerife

A Whole House — Just for You

Ocean Deck House is a fully private, standalone holiday home — not an apartment, not a shared building. You get the entire property to yourself, with your own front door, your own garden, and your own parking spot right outside.

The house comfortably sleeps up to 4 guests across 2 bedrooms, making it ideal for couples, small families, or two friends travelling together.

Located in the peaceful village of Abades on the south-east coast of Tenerife, you're surrounded by volcanic landscape, minutes from the sea, and far from the noise of the tourist resorts.

Room by Room

🛏️ Bedroom 1

The main bedroom features a comfortable double bed, wardrobe, and natural light. Bed linen and pillows are included. The room is quiet and designed for a restful night's sleep after a day of exploring.

🛏️ Bedroom 2

The second bedroom is perfect for two more guests or children. Flexible sleeping arrangements available. Wardrobe and storage included.

🚿 Bathroom

One full private bathroom with shower, toilet and washbasin. Towels are provided. Hot water available at all times.

🍳 Fully Equipped Kitchen

Cook like you're at home. The kitchen includes a hob, oven, fridge, microwave, toaster, coffee maker, and a full set of pots, pans, plates, glasses and cutlery. Perfect for self-catering holidays.

🌿 Private Garden & Terrace

Step outside into your own private outdoor space. The terrace and garden are fully enclosed and private — perfect for morning coffee, al fresco dining, or simply relaxing in the Tenerife sun. On clear days you can see Mount Teide from here.

Work Remotely from Paradise

Ocean Deck House is equipped with high-speed fiber internet — reliable enough for video calls, large file transfers, and streaming. Whether you're a digital nomad or just want to stay connected, you're covered.

The peaceful environment of Abades, combined with fast internet and a private workspace, makes this an ideal destination for workcations.
