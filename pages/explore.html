Explore Abades

South Tenerife's most authentic and unspoiled coastal village

Welcome to Abades

Abades is a small, quiet fishing village on the south-east coast of Tenerife — a world away from the busy tourist resorts of Playa de las Américas or Los Cristianos. Here you'll find crystal-clear water, volcanic landscape, authentic local life, and some of the island's best snorkeling.

The village sits in a sheltered natural bay that protects it from the trade winds, making it one of the calmest and warmest swimming spots on the island — even in winter.

What to Do in & Around Abades

🤿 Snorkeling

The bay at Abades is one of the best snorkeling spots in Tenerife. The volcanic rock formations create natural reefs teeming with parrotfish, wrasse, eels, octopus and more. The water is calm and visibility is exceptional.

Full Snorkeling Guide →

🏚️ Sanatorio de Abona

One of Tenerife's most mysterious landmarks — an abandoned tuberculosis hospital from the 1930s, perched dramatically on the cliffs above Abades. A must-see for history lovers and urban explorers.

History & Visitor Guide →

🌋 Mount Teide

Spain's highest peak and one of the world's most visited volcanoes. On clear days you can see Teide from the terrace of Ocean Deck House. A day trip to the Teide National Park is unmissable.

Stargazing at Teide →

🐋 Whale & Dolphin Watching

The waters off South Tenerife are home to resident pilot whales and bottlenose dolphins year-round. Boat trips depart from Los Cristianos and Los Gigantes — a 30-minute drive from Abades.

Whale Watching Guide →

🍽️ Arico Village & Local Food

Just 10 minutes inland, the village of Arico Nuevo offers authentic Canarian restaurants, local markets, and a glimpse into traditional Tenerife life. Try papas arrugadas, fresh fish, and local wine.

Local Food Guide →

⭐ Stargazing

Tenerife has some of the darkest and clearest skies in Europe. The area around Abades and Teide is perfect for stargazing. The Milky Way is visible to the naked eye on clear nights.

Best Time to Visit

South Tenerife enjoys over 300 sunny days per year and average temperatures between 20°C and 28°C. There is no bad time to visit — but here's what to expect:

  • Winter (Dec–Feb): 20–22°C, quiet, great for snorkeling and hiking. Best Teide views.
  • Spring (Mar–May): 22–25°C, wildflowers, perfect weather, fewer tourists.
  • Summer (Jun–Aug): 26–30°C, busier, warm sea, great for swimming.
  • Autumn (Sep–Nov): 24–27°C, warm sea, quieter, excellent value.
